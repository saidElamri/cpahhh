## FAQ Section

**H2: Frequently Asked Questions**

### General Questions

**Q: What makes [Brand Name] different from other luxury beauty brands?**
A: We combine scientific innovation with botanical wisdom, maintaining uncompromising quality while being 100% cruelty-free and sustainably sourced.

**Q: Do you ship to my country?**
A: We deliver to all major markets including US, Canada, Western Europe, and GCC countries. International shipping with luxury packaging is complimentary on orders over $500.

**Q: What is your return policy?**
A: We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely delighted with your purchase, contact our concierge team for a complimentary return.

### Product Questions

**Q: Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?**
A: Yes, all our formulations are dermatologist-tested and designed for all skin types, including sensitive skin. We always recommend a patch test for new products.

**Q: How long do products last after opening?**
A: Most products have a 6-month shelf life after opening. Each product includes a PAO (Period After Opening) symbol for reference.

**Q: Are your products vegan?**
A: Many of our formulations are vegan, but some contain high-performance ingredients like beeswax or honey. All products are clearly labeled.

### Order & Shipping

**Q: How quickly will I receive my order?**
A: Express shipping is complimentary on orders over $300. Delivery times vary by location: 2-3 business days within the US, 3-5 business days to Europe, 2-4 business days to GCC.

**Q: Can I modify or cancel my order?**
A: Orders can be modified within 2 hours of placement. After that, we process orders immediately for quality and freshness.

**Q: Do you offer gift wrapping and personalization?**
A: Yes, we offer complimentary luxury gift wrapping with personalized messages. Gift options are available at checkout.
