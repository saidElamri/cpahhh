How to Wear Nipple Pasties Under Sheer Tops

1

Prepare Your Skin

Ensure skin is clean, dry, and free from lotions or oils. Pat completely dry for maximum adhesion.

2

Apply with Care

Remove protective film and center the pastie over your nipple. Press firmly for 10 seconds, smoothing from center outward.

3

Remove & Store

Gently peel from edges. Rinse with mild soap and lukewarm water. Air dry adhesive-side up, then return to storage case.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these nipple pasties for backless dresses?

Absolutely! Our silicone nipple covers are specifically designed to work with backless, strapless, and low-cut styles. The self-adhesive design requires no straps or support, making them ideal for wedding dresses, evening gowns, and summer attire.

How long do reusable nipple pasties last?

With proper care, each pair lasts 50-100 wears. After each use, simply wash with mild soap and water, air dry adhesive-side up, and store in the included protective case. Adhesion may decrease over time but can often be restored with proper cleaning.

Will they show under tight or light-colored clothing?

Our ultra-thin tapered edges are virtually undetectable under all fabrics, including white, nude, and tight-fitting clothes. The matte finish eliminates shine, and the skin-tone options ensure complete invisibility under sheer materials.
