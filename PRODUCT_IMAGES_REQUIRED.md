## What Are Nipple Pasties?
Nipple pasties (also called nipple covers) are small, reusable silicone adhesive covers that women wear to hide their nipples under clothing without wearing a bra. They are:
- Small circular or petal-shaped silicone discs
- Self-adhesive on one side
- Reusable (can be washed and reused)
- Invisible under clothing
- Used for backless dresses, sheer tops, etc.
